EYO Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-09

1. Overview

EYO is a time management app for independent professionals. It helps users create projects, track time, add manual entries, review reports, and export time data when needed.

2. Local-First Data Storage

EYO stores app data locally on the user's device by default. This may include projects, client names entered by the user, hourly rates, time entries, start and end times, notes, billable and non-billable status, reports generated from local entries, app settings, and Pro status.

By default, this data is not uploaded to an EYO server.

3. No Account Required

EYO can be used without creating an account. The current version does not require user registration, login credentials, email-based account creation, or cloud profile creation.

4. Data Collection

EYO stores the time tracking data entered by the user locally on the device by default. Data entered into the app remains stored locally on the device unless the user chooses to export or share it.

EYO may also collect limited technical and usage information through third-party services such as Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. This information is used to understand how the app is used, monitor app stability, diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve performance.

5. Analytics and Crash Diagnostics

EYO may use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics, services provided by Google Firebase, to monitor app performance, understand general usage patterns, and identify crashes or technical issues.

These services may collect technical information such as device type, operating system version, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, performance data, session information, and general usage events.

This information is used only to improve the app, maintain reliability, and understand which features are useful to users.

On Android, Firebase Analytics may use the advertising identifier for analytics and attribution purposes where available. EYO does not use the advertising identifier to display ads, sell user data, or share data with advertising partners.

6. Data Sharing

EYO does not sell user data.

EYO does not use third-party advertising networks and does not share user data for advertising purposes.

EYO may share limited technical analytics and crash diagnostic data with service providers such as Google Firebase for app stability, analytics, and performance monitoring.

The user may choose to export or share data manually, for example by creating a CSV or PDF export and using the device share sheet.

7. CSV and PDF Export

EYO may allow users to export time tracking data as CSV or PDF. Exports may include project names, client names entered by the user, time entry dates, start and end times, durations, notes, billable status, hourly rates, calculated values, summaries, and report periods. Export files are only created when the user chooses to export or share them.

8. Pro Access and In-App Purchases

Some EYO features may be limited to Pro users, such as monthly reports, CSV export, PDF export, or unlimited projects. Pro access may be provided through official in-app purchases or subscriptions offered by the app stores, such as Google Play or the Apple App Store.

Payment, subscription management, cancellations, and refunds are handled by the relevant app store provider. EYO does not store full payment card details.

EYO may store purchase status locally on the device and may use app store purchase information to determine whether Pro features should be available.

9. Cloud Sync and Backup

The current version does not include cloud sync by default. The current version may not include automatic backup or restore features. Users are responsible for keeping any exported files safe.

10. Device Permissions

EYO may use standard device features when required by app functionality, such as sharing exported files through the operating system share sheet.

EYO may request notification permission to show timer-related notifications, such as an indication that a timer is running or timer-related reminders. Users can enable or disable notifications in their device settings.

EYO does not request unnecessary permissions for core time tracking functionality.

11. Data Deletion

Because EYO stores time tracking data locally by default, users can delete app data by deleting entries, projects where supported, or removing the app and its local data from the device.

Analytics and crash diagnostic data collected through Firebase are handled according to Google Firebase's data processing and retention practices.

12. Children

EYO is not designed for children and is intended for productivity and professional use.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as EYO evolves. Changes may be required when new features are added, such as cloud sync, account creation, analytics, crash reporting, or additional subscription options.

14. Contact

For support, feedback, or privacy-related questions, contact: eyo.app.support@gmail.com
